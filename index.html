Short Bio:

I grew up in North Carolina and was an engineering student turned math major. Currently I'm a community college faculty member. I'm often too hard on myself, sometimes judgmental, but always trying to make the world just a little better everyday. In the process I strive to be both present and vulnerable. An ever-curious questioner, my CliftonStrengths signature themes are ideation, intellection, input, futuristic, and strategic. I've taken the Myers-Briggs twice, with different results: INFJ, INTP.
